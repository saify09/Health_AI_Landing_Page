How It Works

From Submission
to Decision — Automated

The Prior Authorization platform in four steps.

01
Provider Submits

Provider completes a 5-step guided wizard. Patient info, clinical criteria, diagnosis codes, and supporting documents — all in one flow.

02
AI Analyzes

The RAG-based AI Engine extracts clinical context, checks against payer guidelines, and scores the case for auto-approval or human review.

03
Reviewer Decides

Complex cases route to a clinical reviewer with full AI recommendations, confidence scores, and guideline citations pre-loaded.

04
Decision Delivered

Approval letters generated instantly. Members and providers notified in real-time. Full audit trail maintained for compliance.
